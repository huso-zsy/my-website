插头Changer

#二三纬IP形象设计 #动画设计 #品牌识别

委托：本科毕业设计
时间：2022年4月-6月

“我侄子侄女看的动画片都很时髦，里面都有各种抽象的角色甚至概念，但怎么大部分面向儿童的教育形象大多都是动物或者人形变种， 且风格也固定不变呢?”抱着这个念头，希望尝试一种多变的可能性。“这玩意感觉点像充电头”所以就“将计就计”取名插头， 联系教育主题形成一句“为知识充电”的solgan。

“The cartoons my nieces and nephews watch are all very fashionable. There are all kinds of abstract characters and even concepts in them, but why are most of the educational images for children mostly animal or human mutants, and the style is also fixed?” Want to try a variety of possibilities. “It feels a little bit like a plug,” he said, so he named the plug after the “Entrapment,” and used the term “Plug for knowledge” in conjunction with the educational theme of solgan.
