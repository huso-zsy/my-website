禾马文创

#品牌识别 #三维动画设计 #动态海报

委托：综合设计课程
时间：2021年11月-12月

此作品基于新建立的文创品牌的logo来进行动态的衍生，同时进行了辅助图形的扩展并将其融入PROMO视频。我尝试将原有的Logo进行现行线性连接，这样更有助于在三维空间内形成一个连续灵动的形态，同时为了满足品牌对于文创的广泛性的体现，设计与飘带般的质感为支点，形成三个同样具有柔体性质的辅助形态，可以将其应用在各种品牌的品类宣传中充当呼应品牌调性的元素。

This work is based on the logo of the newly established creative brand to carry on the dynamic derivation, at the same time carries on the auxiliary graphics extension and integrates it into the PROMO video. I tried to create a linear connection between the original Logo, which helped create a continuous and flexible shape within the 3D, and to meet the brand's broad cultural appeal, design and ribbon-like texture as a fulcrum, forming three similar flexible nature of the auxiliary form, can be used in a variety of brands in the category of publicity as a response to the brand tone of the elements.
